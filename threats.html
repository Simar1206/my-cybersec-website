Common Cyber Threats

Know your enemy — understanding threats is the first step to defending against them.

🦠

Malware

Malicious software designed to disrupt, damage, or gain unauthorized access to computer systems. Includes viruses, worms, trojans, and spyware.

🎣

Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity in emails, messages, or fake websites.

💣

Ransomware

A type of malware that encrypts a victim's files and demands a ransom payment to restore access. Has caused billions in damages worldwide.

🕵️

Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) Attacks

An attacker secretly intercepts and possibly alters communication between two parties who believe they are communicating directly.

🔓

Password Attacks

Attempts to crack or steal passwords using brute force, dictionary attacks, or credential stuffing to gain unauthorized access to accounts.
